Half-Life

Dr. Gordon Freeman, a theoretical physicist, must fight his way through the contaminated Black Mesa Research Facility after a catastrophic experiment opens a portal to an alien world. This groundbreaking sci-fi shooter revolutionized the FPS genre with its immersive storytelling, intelligent AI, and physics-based gameplay. Experience the game that defined a generation and spawned one of gaming's most beloved franchises.

Team Fortress

Join one of nine distinct classes in this iconic class-based multiplayer shooter. From the stealthy Spy to the massive Heavy, each character brings unique weapons, personalities, and playstyles to the battlefield. With its distinctive cartoon art style, hilarious dialogue, and deep team strategies, Team Fortress 2 has remained one of the most beloved online shooters for nearly two decades. Grab your favorite hat and jump into the action!

CSGO

The world's premier tactical first-person shooter enters a new era. Counter-Strike 2 delivers the classic 5v5 gameplay you love—terrorists vs. counter-terrorists—with stunning Source 2 graphics, reactive smoke grenades, and sub-tick updates for unparalleled precision. Plant the bomb, rescue hostages, or eliminate the enemy team in this free-to-play competitive masterpiece that demands skill, strategy, and teamwork.

Portal

Test your mind in Aperture Science's mysterious facility. Armed with the revolutionary Aperture Science Handheld Portal Device, you'll solve increasingly complex puzzles by creating spatial portals—walk through walls, launch across gaps, and manipulate objects in ways never before possible. Guided by the passive-aggressive AI GLaDOS, this award-winning puzzle game combines brilliant design, dark humor, and unforgettable moments. The cake is not a lie!
