F.A.Q.:

Is VALVe working on new games?

Our company has confirmed we are working on new games. In recent years, we have released Half-Life: Alyx (2020), a critically acclaimed VR game and Counter-Strike 2 (2023). VALVe continues to develop and support games like CS2 with regular updates and tournaments.

Where is VALVe located?

VALVe's headquarters is located in Bellevue, Washington, United States. The company also has offices in other locations including Luxembourg, Singapore, and Germany to support its global operations, particularly for Steam.

What is it like to work at VALVe

Here employees choose which projects to work on and are encouraged to move between teams based on their interests and skills. This structure is better explained in our employee handbook.
