About Steam:

Steam is a game platform created by VALVe with the objective of being the main hub for all games. Currently, Steam has more than 30.000 games available of all genres with exclusive discounts, automatic updates and other advantages.

Community

Community is a social hub for gamers integrated into Steam. Every game has its own Hub for sharing all kinds of things, plus Discussions for players to connect. Create or join groups, see what friends are playing, and broadcast gameplay—all in one place.

Hardware

We have a lineup of devices bringing Steam to new spaces. Coming Soon: the Steam Machine (4K console), Steam Frame (wireless VR headset), and a redesigned Steam Controller. All run on SteamOS and work seamlessly together.

Our Features:

Chat

Speak with friends or in voice and text channels without leaving Steam. You can send gifs, videos, tweets, etc...

Availability

Steam can be accessed in iOS, Android and also the main computer app.

Multilingual

Creating a global community is important to us, and that is why our app is available in more than 28 languages and more in the near future.

Early Access

Discover, play and integrate yourself in games during their evolution. Be the first to see what's coming and become part of the creative process
